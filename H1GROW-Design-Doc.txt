================================================================================
DESIGN PHILOSOPHY
================================================================================

Inspired by VengeanceUI's brutalist aesthetic: dark, high-contrast, 
unapologetically bold. This is not a soft, friendly SaaS page. This is for 
creators who want to make real money and aren't afraid of standing out.

CORE AESTHETIC
--------------
• BRUTALIST/INDUSTRIAL: Raw, geometric, functional
• HIGH CONTRAST: Pure blacks, sharp whites, electric accent colors
• AGGRESSIVE TYPOGRAPHY: Large, bold, in-your-face headlines
• MINIMAL BUT IMPACTFUL: Every element has a purpose, no fluff
• EDGY, NOT POLISHED: We're disruptors, not corporate

================================================================================
COLOR PALETTE
================================================================================

PRIMARY COLORS
--------------
• #000000 - Pure Black: Main background
• #FFFFFF - Pure White: Primary text, high contrast elements

ACCENT COLORS
-------------
• #00FF00 or #00FF94 - Electric/Neon Green: CTAs, highlights, success states
• #FF0000 or #FF0055 - Hot Red/Pink: Attention grabbers, warnings, problem 
  callouts
• Optional: Electric Blue for secondary accents if needed

NEUTRAL GRAYS
-------------
• #1A1A1A - Dark Gray: Card backgrounds
• #333333 - Medium Gray: Borders, dividers
• #888888 - Light Gray: Secondary text

COLOR USAGE GUIDELINES
-----------------------
• Black background with white text as foundation
• Neon green for all CTAs and positive actions
• Hot red/pink for problem sections and urgency elements
• Geometric color blocks to break up sections

================================================================================
TYPOGRAPHY
================================================================================

DISPLAY FONT (HEADLINES)
-------------------------
Font Options: Druk Wide Bold, Monument Extended, or Sequel Sans

• Ultra-bold, condensed, geometric
• All caps for major headlines
• Usage: H1GROW logo, section titles, hero headline

BODY FONT
---------
Font Options: Neue Haas Grotesk, Suisse Int'l, or Inter

• Clean, readable, modern
• Usage: Paragraphs, descriptions, CTAs

TYPOGRAPHIC HIERARCHY
----------------------
• H1 (Hero): 80-120px, all caps, ultra-bold
• H2 (Section Titles): 48-64px, all caps, bold
• H3 (Card Titles): 24-32px, semi-bold
• Body: 16-18px, regular weight

KEY PRINCIPLE: Extreme scale jumps - no subtle sizing

================================================================================
LAYOUT & GRID
================================================================================

STRUCTURE
---------
• Full-width sections with max-width containers (1200px)
• Asymmetric layouts - offset elements, diagonal cuts
• Generous whitespace - let elements breathe
• Grid-breaking elements - images/cards that bleed outside containers
• Sharp geometric shapes - rectangles, triangles, diagonal lines as dividers

SECTION DIVIDERS
----------------
• Thick horizontal lines (4-8px)
• Diagonal slashes cutting across sections
• Geometric shape overlays
• Negative space manipulation

================================================================================
COMPONENT DESIGN
================================================================================

BUTTONS
-------
• Hard edges, no rounded corners (border-radius: 0)
• Thick borders (2-3px)
• All caps text
• Hover: Inverted colors with hard transition (no smooth fade)
• Primary CTA: Neon green background, black text
• Secondary: Black background, white border, white text

CARDS
-----
• Sharp rectangles, no rounded corners
• Subtle borders (1-2px, #333)
• Hover: Hard shadow drop, slight position shift
• Black or very dark gray backgrounds
• White text with colored accents

FORMS
-----
• Brutalist input fields - thick borders, square corners
• Labels inside or aggressively positioned outside
• High contrast states (focus = neon green border)

ICONS
-----
• Geometric, line-based
• Minimal detail
• High contrast (white or neon colors)

================================================================================
ANIMATION & MOTION
================================================================================

PRINCIPLES
----------
• Hard cuts over smooth transitions
• Aggressive timing - fast, snappy (100-200ms)
• Glitch effects on hover (optional, if not overdone)
• Parallax scrolling for depth
• Reveal animations - elements slide/cut in sharply

SPECIFIC EFFECTS
----------------
• Hero text: Staggered reveal with hard cuts
• Section scroll: Elements snap into view
• Button hover: Color inversion with no transition
• Card hover: Hard shadow drop + 4px position shift

DO NOT USE
----------
• Smooth ease-in-out curves
• Bouncy animations
• Gradients (unless very deliberate and harsh)
• Soft shadows

SECTION 1: HERO

• Full viewport height
• Headline: "MONETIZE YOUR CONTENT" - 100px, all caps, white, hard edges

  monetization they deserve" - 24px, light gray
• CTA button: Neon green, black text, thick border, all caps "GET STARTED"
• Background: Pure black with subtle geometric pattern or grid
• Accent: Neon green underline or block behind key word

SECTION 2: PROBLEM
------------------
• Title: "THE PROBLEM" - red/hot pink color
• 3 problem cards laid out in grid
• Each card: Black background, red accent border on left edge, white text
• Icons: Simple geometric shapes in red
• Visual treatment: Diagonal slash dividing this section from hero

Problem Cards:
1. Low value brands
2. No ad revenue
3. Physical products = inventory risk, delivery headaches, thin margins

SECTION 3: SOLUTION
-------------------
• Title: "THE SOLUTION" - neon green color
• Main copy: Large text (32-40px) explaining personalized software
• Highlighted phrase: "TAILORED TO YOUR BRAND" in neon green block
• Description: Personalized software tailored to your brand tone and audience
• Visual: Mockup of software product in harsh lighting (optional)

SECTION 4: HOW IT WORKS (3 STEPS)
----------------------------------
• Numbered steps: Large geometric numbers (01, 02, 03)
• Layout: Horizontal timeline or vertical stack
• Each step: Number in neon green, title in white (all caps), description 
  in gray
• Arrow/connectors: Sharp geometric arrows between steps

Step 1: Click the button
Step 2: Explain what you want
Step 3: Get your products, risk free

SECTION 5: REFER A FRIEND
--------------------------
• Title: "BRING YOUR CREW"
• Simple explanation of referral benefits
• CTA: Share link button in neon green
• Visual: Simple graphic showing network effect

SECTION 6: FAQ
--------------
• Accordion style with hard edges
• Closed state: White text on black
• Open state: Neon green accent bar appears
• Transition: Hard snap open/close (no smooth accordion)

SECTION 7: FINAL CTA
--------------------
• Large section with centered content
• Headline: "READY TO GROW?" - huge, all caps
• Form fields: Name, Email, Instagram handle, Follower count
• Submit button: Neon green, oversized
• Urgency text: "Limited spots - 10 creators this month" in red

SECTION 8: FOOTER
-----------------
• Minimal, clean
• White text on black
• Links separated by slashes: "About / Contact / Privacy"
• Small H1GROW logo

================================================================================
RESPONSIVE DESIGN
================================================================================

• Mobile: Stack everything vertically but maintain brutalist aesthetic
• Keep type hierarchy even on small screens
• Buttons go full-width but maintain thick borders
• No compromise on boldness

================================================================================
UNIQUE VISUAL ELEMENTS
================================================================================

• Geometric shapes as section backgrounds (triangles, rectangles)
• Diagonal cuts between sections
• Thick accent lines (neon green or red) used sparingly for emphasis
• Grid overlay on hero or background (very subtle)
• Glitch effect on logo or key headlines (very subtle, on hover only)
